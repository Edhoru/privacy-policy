# Privacy Policy for AR Canvas

**Last updated: March 26, 2026**

AR Canvas does not collect, store, or share your personal information directly.

## Camera

AR Canvas requires access to your device's camera to provide its core augmented reality feature — placing artwork and images into your real-world environment. Camera data is processed entirely on-device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third party by us.

## Third-Party Services

### Google AdMob (Google Mobile Ads SDK)

The free version of AR Canvas displays ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect certain information from your device to serve, measure, and improve ads, and to help with security and fraud prevention. This information may include:

- Device identifiers (e.g. IDFA, if you have granted permission)
- Approximate location derived from IP address
- App interaction and usage data
- Diagnostic information

You can review Google's data practices here:
- Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- Google AdMob: https://support.google.com/admob/

### RevenueCat

AR Canvas uses RevenueCat to manage auto-renewable subscriptions. RevenueCat processes your Apple purchase receipt and may collect:

- A pseudonymous user identifier
- Purchase and entitlement history
- Device and app version information

RevenueCat uses this data solely to validate and manage your subscription. RevenueCat Privacy Policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

### Apple CloudKit

AR Canvas fetches image and category content from Apple's CloudKit public database. This is a read-only operation used to deliver app content; no personal data is written to or stored in CloudKit by this app.

## Subscriptions

AR Canvas offers an optional auto-renewable subscription ("Gold") that removes ads and unlocks premium content. Subscriptions are billed and managed by Apple through your Apple ID. We do not have access to your payment information. You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time in your Apple ID account settings.

## Children's Privacy

AR Canvas is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
