# Privacy Policy for Cheerbit

**Last updated: April 28, 2026**

Cheerbit is designed with your privacy in mind. All your affirmations, recordings, and preferences are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to our servers.

## Data Stored on Your Device

Cheerbit stores the following data exclusively on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework:

- Custom affirmations you write
- Voice recordings you create in Custom Studio
- Speech transcriptions of your recordings
- Your mood preferences and daily streak
- Reminder settings

None of this data leaves your device or is accessible to us.

## Microphone

Cheerbit requests microphone access solely to record your voice affirmations in Custom Studio. Recordings are stored locally on your device and are never uploaded or shared.

## Speech Recognition

When you record a voice affirmation, Cheerbit uses Apple's Speech Recognition framework to generate a text transcription. This audio data is processed by Apple according to [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/). We do not receive or store this data on any server.

## Subscriptions

Cheerbit offers optional in-app purchases and auto-renewable subscriptions (weekly, monthly, yearly) managed entirely by Apple through your Apple ID. We do not have access to your payment information or billing details. You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time in your Apple ID account settings.

## Widgets

Cheerbit includes a home screen widget. Widget data (your current affirmation and theme) is shared between the app and the widget extension using an App Group stored locally on your device. No data is sent externally.

## Third-Party Services

Cheerbit does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. No data is shared with third parties except as described under Speech Recognition above (Apple's own framework).

## Children's Privacy

Cheerbit is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. The date at the top of this page reflects the most recent update.
