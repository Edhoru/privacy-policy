# Privacy Policy for Collect

Effective date: February 25, 2026

Collect ("we", "us", "our") provides software to help hosts manage memberships, payment tracking, and payment reminders for their organizations.

This Privacy Policy explains what data we process, why we process it, and how users can contact us.

## 1. Scope

This policy applies to:

- Hosts who create and manage organizations in Collect.
- Members added by hosts to receive payment-related communications.
- Visitors of Collect web properties.

## 2. What data we process

We process data provided by hosts, members, or system integrations, including:

- Account and profile data:
  - Host name
  - Host phone/WhatsApp number
  - Organization name
  - Locale and timezone
- Member management data:
  - Member name
  - Member phone/WhatsApp number
  - Billing frequency and due dates
  - Payment status records (for example: pending, paid, overdue)
- Payment method instructions configured by hosts:
  - Cash
  - Bank transfer details (for example CLABE)
  - Wallet/payment links
  - Other payment instructions entered by host
- Technical and usage data:
  - Request metadata (for security and reliability)
  - Message dispatch logs
  - Error logs

## 3. What we do not process

- We do not process card payments directly in Collect.
- We do not store full card numbers, CVV, or card expiration details.
- We do not act as a payment processor for member-to-host payments.

## 4. Why we process data

We process data to:

- Create and manage host organizations.
- Manage members and payment tracking workflows.
- Send or automate payment reminders through connected messaging providers.
- Operate the service securely and reliably.
- Comply with legal obligations.

## 5. Legal bases (where applicable)

Depending on jurisdiction, processing may be based on:

- Contract performance (providing the service requested by hosts).
- Legitimate interests (service security, fraud prevention, diagnostics).
- Consent or instruction from the host for member communications.
- Compliance with legal obligations.

## 6. Third-party services and processors

Collect uses third-party infrastructure and service providers, including:

- Supabase (database and backend data services)
- Cloudflare (edge/network and API security)
- n8n (automation workflows)
- Messaging providers (for example Meta WhatsApp Cloud API and/or Twilio WhatsApp)
- LemonSqueezy (host subscription billing for Collect)

These providers may process data under their own terms and privacy policies.

## 7. Messaging and reminders

Hosts are responsible for:

- Ensuring they have the right to add member contact details.
- Using reminders in compliance with applicable laws and platform rules.
- Managing message content and cadence appropriately.

Collect may store reminder and message delivery metadata (for example sent/failed status, timestamps, provider IDs) for operational and audit purposes.

## 8. Data retention

We retain data for as long as reasonably necessary to:

- Provide the service,
- Maintain records requested by hosts,
- Resolve disputes,
- Enforce agreements,
- Comply with legal obligations.

Retention periods may vary by data type and account status.

## 9. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data, including access controls and secured infrastructure components. No system can guarantee absolute security.

## 10. International processing

Data may be processed in countries other than where users are located, depending on infrastructure and provider regions.

## 11. Children

Collect is not directed to children and is intended for business/organizational use. Hosts are responsible for lawful handling of any member data they submit.

## 12. Your rights

Depending on jurisdiction, users may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of personal data. Requests should be sent to the contact below.

## 13. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Updates are effective when posted with a revised effective date.
