# Privacy Policy for Vía Crucis

Effective date: April 2, 2026

Vía Crucis ("we", "us", "our") is an iOS app for reading and praying the Stations of the Cross.

This Privacy Policy explains what data is processed, where it is stored, and how to contact us.

## 1. Data we process

Vía Crucis does not require account creation and does not use a backend server for personal user data.

The app processes the following information on your device:

- Reading progress (for example, last opened stage) when you enable resume behavior.
- Reading preferences (such as appearance mode, text size, font style, line spacing, and related display settings).
- Optional reading behavior settings (for example, keeping the screen awake while reading).
- App content files (stations and prayers) bundled with the app.

## 2. Where data is stored

Data is stored locally on your device using iOS app storage.

We do not upload this data to our own servers.

## 3. Tracking, analytics, and ads

Vía Crucis does not use third-party advertising SDKs and does not perform cross-app tracking.

At this time, we do not use third-party analytics services for user profiling.

## 4. Third-party services

The app is distributed through Apple App Store. Apple may process limited diagnostic, billing, or platform data according to Apple's own policies.

- Apple Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

## 5. Data retention and control

Because app data is local to your device:

- You can reset behavior by changing settings in-app.
- You can remove app data by deleting the app from your device.

## 6. Children's privacy

Vía Crucis is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## 7. Security

We rely on iOS platform security and reasonable app-level safeguards. No method of storage or transmission is 100% secure.

## 8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates are effective when posted with a revised effective date.
