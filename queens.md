# Privacy Policy for Queens

Queens does not collect, store, or share personal information directly.

## Third-party services (Ads)
Queens uses Google AdMob (Google Mobile Ads SDK) to display ads. AdMob may collect certain information from your device to provide, measure, and improve ads, and to help with security/fraud prevention. This information may include device identifiers, approximate location (from IP address), app interaction data, and diagnostics, depending on your configuration and how the SDK is used.

You can learn more here:
- Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- Google AdMob: https://support.google.com/admob/
